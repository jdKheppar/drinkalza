FAQs

Why did we make ALZA?

Long story short, a couple of friends bought mass-market hard seltzer. After a day of partying on the beach, we all got stomachaches. We started looking at the ingredients and realized that we couldn’t identify what we were actually drinking. So we decided to create a 100% all-natural tequila soda with complete ingredient transparency.

What does it taste like?

Since we only use natural ingredients, it pretty much tastes like a tequila soda with splash of flavor, not to sweet but just right! Ever had that perfect tequila soda with a splash of lime? That’s what we are going for.

What is the difference between ALZA and hard seltzer?

ALZA uses premium blanco tequila as its alcoholic base. Most hard seltzers get their alcohol from fermented sugarcane or from a malt based alcohol.

Can I buy ALZA online?

Yes, we are actively working with our production and distribution team to get ALZA sold online! We want you to feel like you’re getting a good value, so we encourage you to buy 2 cases at a time so that your shipping cost is cut in half! Also, look for us in a local store or get it delivered through Drizzly, GoPuff, or Minibar!

Is ALZA gluten free? Vegan Friendly? Kosher? Pareve?

YES, YES, YES, YES!!! For a copy of our kosher certification, please email us at salud@drinkalza.com

How much Tequila do you use?

ALZA is made with premium blanco tequila and is 5.5%  ABV, or Alcohol By Volume!

Does ALZA contain any know allergens?

ALZA does not contain any FDA recognized major food allergens nor is it produced in any facility that produces or packages any ingredients that are identified as allergens. We got you covered!

How long can ALZA sit on the shelf?

Our shelf life testing indicates that ALZA is good for up to 2 years, however we recommend you drink it within 3 months to ensure flavors are at peak yumminess!

Will ALZA sponsor our team or our event?

If your event is anywhere in South Florida, we are happy to sponsor your event. We sponsor the following: Charities and Fundraisers, Not For Profits, Religious Gatherings, Launch Parties and Promotional Parties. Email us at salud@drinkalza.com for more information.

We will absolutely sponsor your team. Contact us!

I'd like to learn more about your distillery!

All of Destiladora Del Valle de Tequila Tequila’s are created using traditional methods of manufacturing, acquire the best agaves from the region of Tequila and the highlands and lowlands of Jalisco, as well as combining the baking processes of both traditional ovens covered in stone masonry and autoclaves. Owners Celia and Michael have a combined experience of 72 years to create an ultra smooth tequila for its customers. The main goal was to create a source of employment in the region that bear the name of high quality Tequila.
